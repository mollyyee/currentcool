Home-furnishing is a complex and cumbersome process. It could involve different retailers, matching furniture, pursuing a certain feel, budgeting, and factoring in the opinions of other housemates. My job was to make furnishing easier.
Printz is a start-up. How do I visualize a credible and trustworthy brand?
I aimed to give Printz a news and curated material look.
TYPOGRAPHY
A classic serif to emulate the news balanced with a rounder sans-serif font for a modern look.
COLOR
Printz, being an image-heavy site, neutral grays that allow the colors of tailored images to inform each user's unique feed.
WORDING
Replaced initial word choices like "blog" and "featured products" with "selections" and "journal"/"lookbook."
