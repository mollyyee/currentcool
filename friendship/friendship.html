RESUME

On Friendship
A culmination of thoughts and thanks for the ones I've met so far.
Project Type
Digital illustration
Timeline
October 23rd, 2017
A part of a 100 days of creativity challenge, this illustration is one of my favorites. I've learned that creativity is the result of two things. The first is the skills and personal experiences of the maker, which frame how inspiration is seen and what can be created. The second is the intersection of circumstances, thoughts, and inspiration.
These 100 days, I've been honing my visual skills and seeking out inspirational work.
Friendship is a deliberate accident.
I'd been thinking that moments are like intersections, where people, places and a point in time collide. These intersections are usually a turbulent sea of other voices, other people, and other hands.
Without realizing it, people can be good at weaving through the crowd. Amidst the others, they choose each other. In this way, friendship is born out of accident and sustained by choice.
